Our Story

Two dogs,
one mission.

Good Dog Roasters started the way most good things do — with a love for something simple done right. We roast every batch by hand in South Florida, paying attention to every temperature curve and timing detail so you don't have to.

Our two dogs keep us company through every roast, and they're the reason behind the name. Great coffee should be honest, fresh, and worth looking forward to.

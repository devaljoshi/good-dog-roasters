The Artisan Drops

Artisan Drops are small-batch coffees roasted by hand right here in Palm Beach County. Each drop features a single origin or limited blend — a different coffee every time, with a full tasting profile, roaster's notes, and a Good Dog story featured on the bag.

Every drop runs in three phases: Registration (free, no commitment — enter for a chance to buy), Selection (we pick winners by a mix of loyalty and random draw and notify by email), and a Purchase Window (selected winners claim their bag via Shopify). Once the window closes, that coffee is gone — we don't restock.

Everyday Collection: Roasted and fulfilled by our commercial partner. Roast dates vary — we recommend brewing within 2–4 weeks of receipt for best flavor.

Shipping & Returns

Our artisan roasts are made in our Palm Beach County kitchen. Under Florida's Cottage Food Law (Florida Statute 500.80), home-roasted food products can only be sold directly to consumers within Florida — interstate shipping isn't permitted under this exemption.

Our Everyday Collection ships to all 50 states. As our artisan operation grows and we obtain a commercial food establishment license, we'll open artisan shipping nationwide — newsletter subscribers will hear first. See our full Shipping Policy for details.

Artisan drops: Ships within 48 hours of roast. Delivery within Florida is typically 2–5 business days.

Everyday Collection: Processed within 1–3 business days, delivered in 3–7 business days depending on your location. You'll receive a tracking number as soon as your order ships.

Coffee is a perishable product, so all sales are final — we can't accept returns for taste preference. That said, if your order arrived damaged, defective, or incorrect, we'll make it right. Email us at hello@good-dog-roasters.com within 7 days of delivery with a photo and your order number.

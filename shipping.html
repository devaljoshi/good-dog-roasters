Shipping Policy

Two collections, two shipping models. Here's what to expect for each.

Artisan Collection — Florida only

Our Artisan Collection is roasted by hand in small batches in our Palm Beach County kitchen. Under Florida's Cottage Food Law (Florida Statute 500.80), home-roasted food products may only be sold directly to consumers within the state of Florida. Artisan drops ship to Florida addresses only — we cannot ship artisan coffee outside of Florida at this time.

Every bag in an Artisan Drop is labeled: "Made in a cottage food operation that is not subject to Florida's food safety regulations."

Artisan shipping timeline

  • Orders ship within 48 hours of roast date — we roast on drop day and get bags out the door fast
  • Delivery within Florida: typically 2–5 business days via USPS or UPS
  • You will receive a tracking number by email once your order ships

Artisan drop windows

Artisan drops open and close within a single day. Orders placed during the live window are fulfilled in the order received. We do not hold orders or accept late requests once a drop closes — every bag is roasted for that specific drop and there is no surplus inventory.

Everyday Collection — ships nationwide

Our Everyday Collection (Single Origin coffees, Blends, Sample Packs, and Tea) is fulfilled by a licensed commercial roaster through our fulfillment partner. These coffees ship to all 50 U.S. states.

Everyday shipping timeline

  • Orders are processed and shipped within 1–3 business days
  • Standard shipping: 3–7 business days depending on your location
  • Expedited options may be available at checkout
  • You will receive a tracking number by email once your order ships

Shipping rates

Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight. We do not mark up shipping — you pay what the carrier charges.

P.O. Boxes

We can ship to P.O. Boxes via USPS. If your order is being fulfilled by UPS or FedEx, a physical address is required.

International shipping

We do not currently ship outside the United States.

Lost or delayed packages

Once a package is in the carrier's hands, delivery timing is outside our control. If your tracking shows your package is stuck or has been lost, contact us at hello@good-dog-roasters.com and we will work with the carrier on your behalf.

Why artisan coffee is Florida-only — and what's coming

As our artisan operation grows, we intend to obtain a commercial food establishment license, which will allow us to ship hand-roasted artisan coffee to all 50 states. When that happens, we'll announce it to our newsletter list first. Join the newsletter to be the first to know.

Contact us

Questions about your order? Email us at hello@good-dog-roasters.com. We respond within 2 business days.

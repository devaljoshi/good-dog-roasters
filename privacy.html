Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information at Good Dog Roasters.

Who we are

Good Dog Roasters is a small-batch artisan coffee roasting company based in Palm Beach County, Florida. Our website is good-dog-roasters.com, and our online shop is hosted at good-dog-roasters.myshopify.com. When we say "we," "us," or "our" in this policy, we mean Good Dog Roasters.

What information we collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Email address (when you join our drop list)
  • Name, shipping address, billing address, and payment information (when you make a purchase through our Shopify store)
  • Any information you include when contacting us via email or our contact form
  • Your name, email, dog's name, story text, and optional photo if you submit a Good Dog Story via our website

When you submit a Good Dog Story through our website, the information you provide — including your name, email address, your dog's name, your story, and any optional photo — is collected via Netlify Forms and used solely to consider your story for an Artisan Drop illustration feature. We will contact you by email before using your story or image in any published material. We do not share this information with third parties, and it is retained only for as long as necessary to evaluate and respond to your submission.

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website or source
  • IP address (anonymized where possible)

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Notify you about limited-release artisan coffee drops (if you joined our drop list)
  • Process and fulfill orders placed through our Shopify store
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and customer service communications
  • Improve our website experience based on usage patterns
  • Send occasional marketing emails about new products or promotions (you can unsubscribe at any time)

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your data:

  • Shopify — processes payments and fulfills orders. Shopify's privacy policy is available at shopify.com/legal/privacy
  • Netlify — hosts our main website. Netlify's privacy policy is available at netlify.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics — helps us understand website traffic (if enabled). Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy
  • Email service provider — manages our drop list and email communications

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review their policies.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We may use:

  • Essential cookies required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site
  • Third-party cookies from services like Shopify and Google Analytics

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our site.

Data retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. If you join our drop list, we keep your email until you unsubscribe. If you make a purchase, we retain order information as required for accounting, tax, and legal purposes.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any email we send
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by adjusting your browser settings or using browser extensions

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request its deletion.

Why do Artisan Drops only ship within Florida?

Our Artisan Collection is roasted by hand in small batches in our Palm Beach County kitchen. Under Florida's Cottage Food Law (Florida Statute 500.80), home-roasted food products may only be sold directly to consumers within the state of Florida — interstate shipping is not permitted under this exemption. Every Artisan Drop bag is labeled accordingly: "Made in a cottage food operation that is not subject to Florida's food safety regulations."

Our Everyday Collection (dropshipped via Dripshipper) ships nationwide — those coffees are roasted and fulfilled by licensed commercial roasters. As our artisan operation grows, we intend to obtain a commercial food establishment license, which will allow us to ship artisan roasts to all 50 states.

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All purchases are processed through Shopify's secure, PCI-compliant payment infrastructure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it.
